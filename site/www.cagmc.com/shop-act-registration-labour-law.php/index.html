Shop Act Registration

Every small time hotel and shop has to secure the shop and establishment license for the purpose of carrying out business activities. However, this license would vary based on the requirements of the state. For example, the shops and establishments license for Punjab would be different from the procedure for shops and establishments in Uttar Pradesh.

Hence it is crucial for the applicant to understand the Central rules as well as the state rules related to shops and establishments before making an application. Moreover, these rules regulate employees working in the organised sector as well as the unorganised sector. Different working conditions have to be complied by the applicant.

Apart from this, the working conditions and terms of employment are also regulated by this license. Therefore, it is important to adhere to the requirements of the state.

Meaning of Shop and Establishment Registration

Shop and Establishment Act registration is the legal permission to run the business in India. The Shop and Establishment Act is regulated by the Department of Labour. Any shop establishment in India must register under the act within 30 days of establishment. Licence obtained by shop act registration serves as a proof for the existence of the business.

The Registration under shop and Establishment Act is one of the mandatory requirements for all businesses which operate from an establishment or a shop. When you establish your business you can get registration for your Firm in many ways, like:

  • It can be registered as a Business entity in India.
  • It can be registered as Start up in India.
  • It can register as MSME Enterprise.
  • It can be registered under shop and establishment act of the respective state in India.

Which entities require Shop and Establishment Registration?

Not only shops but all the commercial establishment mentioned below are required to get registered itself into shop and registration act:

  • Commercial sector establishments like trading, insurance, or banks etc.
  • Establishments where people are employed to provide service call centre etc.
  • Refreshments house, small cafe, boarding houses, eateries, hotels, Restaurants etc.
  • Entertainment establishments like cinema halls, theatres, amusement parks, etc

When to apply for Shop Act Registration in Kotkapura ?

When a business entity starts employing people whether on contract basis or full-time employees, or to casual workers, etc. then every shop and establishment needs to register itself compulsorily under this Act within 30 days of commencement of work to enforce proper regulations for worker’s rights and work conditions.

  • If in case the same is covered, then he or she is required to abide by the norms and regulations prescribed by the Act for the treatment of their employees.

What should Employer declare at the time of registration?

  • Working hours of employees
  • Number of workers
  • Rest interval of employees
  • Opening and closing hours
  • Health and safety measures
  • Wages for overtime work
  • National and religious holidays etc

Documents required for Shop Act Registration in Kotkapura

  • Photo of the establishment along with Name Board and Director
  • List of directors and management with their ID & Address proof.
  • Details related to the company, employees, their salaries etc.
  • Establishment’s address proof
  • Affidavit
  • Copy of Pan card

How to apply for Shop and establishment registration?

You can apply for shop and establishment registration online by visiting the state government’s website. The application in the format prescribed on the website needs to be submitted with the help of above-mentioned details and documents along with the prescribed fee. When the application is received by the Department of Labour, an inspector will visit your establishment to check the details and will provide the registration certificate/license in accordance with the Act.

What records to be maintained under shop & establishment act?

Under the Shop and Establishment Act, every business has to take approval from Department of Labour and keep up-to-date registers regarding details of employment, fines, deductions and advances, salary and holidays. The requirements may vary from state to state. No Regular return have needed to be filed under this Act Provided files related to annual holidays and number of employees need to be submitted to the office of the Municipal Corporation annually.

Amendment in shop & establishment act registration:

Any changes to the Shop and Establishment Act registration must be intimated to the concerned officer within the prescribed time limit. In case of closure of the Business, the employer is required to intimate the concerned officer and apply for surrender of the registration. The officer after the satisfaction about the correctness of the application, will issue an order cancelling the existing registration.
