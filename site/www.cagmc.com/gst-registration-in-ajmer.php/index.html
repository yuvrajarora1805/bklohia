Goods And Services Tax (GST)Registration In Ajmer

Goods and Service Tax is an Indirect tax(ie,burden shift on the consumer at the end) in India. According to GST law it is mandatory to apply for GST registration when any business fulfill the criteria as prescribed in the law of GST.

GST Registration is kind of process through which a taxpayer get registered under GST act. When a business is registered successfully, a unique number for registration is given to the taxpayer which is known as the Goods and Services Tax Identification Number (GSTIN).

How Many Kind Of GST Registration Are available ?

Kind of GST registration available are as follows:

  • Compulsory RegistrationIn case when the dealer fall under the certain criteria as per GST Act then they must have to take Compulsory Registration under GST irrespective of the amount of turnover they have.
  • Voluntary RegistrationRegistration done on there solely decision not for completion with any GST Act requirements.
  • Registration under Composition SchemeA taxpayer whose turnover is below Rs 1.5 crore can opt out for Composition Scheme. In case of North-Eastern states and Himachal Pradesh, the limit is now Rs 75 lakhs.

What Are Documents Required For Complete The Legal Formalities In GST Registration In Ajmer ?

In order to complete the legal formalities for GST registration documents required are as follows:

  • All the people who are members they need to provide identity proof and photo id for verification.
  • PAN of company which is important and incorporation proof has to be given.
  • There must be a proof of the constitution of the company to ensure its existence, and these documents will be also verified.
  • Proof for the place of business (for this Rent agreement , ownership document are required and electricity bill is compulsory)
  • Bank account details are needed.
  • An authorization form which is signed by an authorized signatory is relevant to the GST registration process.
  • Digital Signature Certificate (DSC) is required

What Is The GST Registration Fees In Ajmer?

The government does not charge any fee for GST registration if the person register for GST online through the government portal (gst.gov.in)

Is there is any kind of penalty for Not Registering Under GST?

If the person does not register under GST, they have to pay a penalty of 10% on the tax due but subject to the amount of Rs 10,000.

Although, in the case person try to evade taxes deliberately, then the person is liable to pay penalty which is 100% of the amount of tax due.
